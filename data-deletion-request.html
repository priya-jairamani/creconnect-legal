Data Deletion Request

Submit a request to permanently delete your personal data from CreConnect

Use this form to request the deletion of your personal data from CreConnect, including any data we obtained from your connected Facebook or Instagram account. All requests are processed within 30 days. You will receive an email confirmation once your data has been deleted.

Submit Your Deletion Request

Use the email address associated with your CreConnect account.
OR

Request via Email

You can also send your deletion request directly to our privacy team. Use the template below:

To: privacy@creconnect.com
Subject: Data Deletion Request — [Your Registered Email]

Dear CreConnect Privacy Team,

I am requesting the permanent deletion of my personal data
from the CreConnect platform.

Account Details:
- Full Name: [Your Full Name]
- Registered Email: [Your Email Address]
- Account Type: [Creator / Brand]

Data to Delete:
[ ] All account data
[ ] Facebook/Instagram data only
[ ] Specific data: [describe]

I confirm that I am the account holder and understand
this deletion is permanent and irreversible.

Thank you.

We will acknowledge your request within 5 business days.

What Happens After You Submit

Within 24 hours:

Your request is received and logged by our privacy team.

Within 5 business days:

We send an acknowledgment email confirming your request and verifying your identity.

Within 30 days:

All requested personal data is permanently deleted from our systems, including data retrieved from Facebook/Instagram APIs.

Completion Notification:

You receive a confirmation email stating that deletion is complete.

Within 90 days:

Your data is purged from all backup systems as backup rotation completes.

Data We May Retain After Deletion

In limited circumstances required by law, we may retain minimal data after a deletion request:

  • Financial records: Transaction data may be retained for up to 7 years per applicable tax and financial regulations.
  • Legal holds: Data subject to ongoing legal proceedings, regulatory inquiries, or active disputes may be held until resolution.
  • Abuse prevention: A minimal hashed record may be retained to prevent re-registration by banned accounts.

Such retained data is kept in a restricted, access-controlled environment and will not be used for marketing, profiling, or any other purpose.
