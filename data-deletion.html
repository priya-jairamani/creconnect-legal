Data Deletion Instructions

Required by Meta Platform Policy  |  Last Updated: June 25, 2026
Meta App Review Required

This page explains how to delete the data that CreConnect has collected from your Facebook and/or Instagram account, as well as all personal data associated with your CreConnect account. You have three ways to request data deletion. All deletion requests are processed within 30 days.

1

Delete via App Settings

The quickest method. Log in to CreConnect and delete your account from your profile settings.

2

Delete via Facebook Settings

Remove CreConnect's access to your Facebook/Instagram data directly from your Facebook account settings.

3

Submit a Deletion Request

Send us a formal data deletion request via our online form or by email for complete data removal.

Method 1: Delete Your Account via CreConnect Settings

This method deletes your CreConnect account and all associated data, including any data obtained from Facebook/Instagram.

  1. Log in to CreConnect

    Visit creconnect.com and sign in to your account using your credentials.

  2. Go to Account Settings

    Click on your profile picture or avatar in the top right corner, then select "Settings" from the dropdown menu.

  3. Navigate to Privacy & Data

    In the Settings menu, select "Privacy & Data" or "Account Management."

  4. Select "Delete Account"

    Scroll to the bottom of the page and click "Delete My Account." You will be asked to confirm this action.

  5. Confirm Deletion

    Enter your password to confirm, then click "Permanently Delete." You will receive a confirmation email at your registered address.

What happens next: Your account will be deactivated immediately. All personal data, including any Facebook/Instagram data we stored, will be permanently deleted within 30 days. Some aggregated, anonymized analytics data may be retained longer for platform improvement purposes.

Method 2: Remove Access via Facebook / Instagram Settings

This method removes CreConnect's permission to access your Facebook or Instagram data going forward. It does not automatically delete data already collected. For full data deletion, also follow Method 1 or Method 3.

To Remove from Facebook:

  1. Go to Facebook Settings

    Visit facebook.com/settings and log in to your account.

  2. Open "Apps and Websites"

    In the left sidebar, click on "Apps and Websites" (under "Security and Login" or directly in the sidebar).

  3. Find CreConnect

    Search for or scroll to find "CreConnect" in the list of connected apps.

  4. Remove the App

    Click the checkbox next to CreConnect, then click "Remove." Check the option to also delete activity if available.

To Remove from Instagram:

  1. Open Instagram Settings

    Go to your Instagram profile, tap the hamburger menu (≡), then select "Settings."

  2. Go to "Apps and Websites"

    Tap "Security" then "Apps and Websites."

  3. Find and Remove CreConnect

    Tap CreConnect in the Active apps list, then tap "Remove."

Note: Removing access via Facebook/Instagram stops future data collection but does not delete data already stored in CreConnect. To request full deletion of stored data, please also submit a formal request using Method 3 below.

Method 3: Submit a Formal Data Deletion Request

If you cannot access your account, or if you want to request deletion of specific data while keeping your account, you can submit a formal data deletion request.

Data We May Retain

In limited circumstances, we may retain certain data after a deletion request for the following reasons:

  • Legal Obligations: Financial transaction records may be retained for up to 7 years as required by tax and financial regulations.
  • Fraud Prevention: Information necessary to prevent re-registration by banned users may be retained in a minimal, hashed form.
  • Dispute Resolution: Data related to pending legal claims or active disputes may be retained until resolution.

Confirmation and Timeline

After submitting a deletion request:

  • You will receive an acknowledgment email within 5 business days confirming receipt of your request.
  • Deletion will be completed within 30 days of verification.
  • You will receive a completion notification once all data has been deleted.
  • Backup data will be purged within 90 days as backup cycles rotate.

If you do not receive a confirmation email, please check your spam folder or contact creconnect2@gmail.com.
